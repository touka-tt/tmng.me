THE DAY OF HARDCORE

VJ Flyer design
2020
Scroll

I designed a flyer for an event to celebrate The Day Of Hardcore(TDOH). In 2020 TDOH will be an online broadcast event and will be entitled "Hadoh Channnel". "Hadoh Channel" is a historical online broadcast event that started in 2006 and it is customary to put an ukiyo-e on the flyer. The TDOH flyer was designed to follow this tradition, but with a modern brush up on the design.
